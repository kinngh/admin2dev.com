Learn Apex Programming

Welcome to 100 Days of Apex! This is a free collection of tutorials, videos, tests and more all designed to help you learn Apex effectively.

This course is aimed at Salesforce Administrators who want to make the switch to a Developer role or expand their skill set beyond Declarative tools.

I’ve tried to pace this course in a way where it takes less than an hour each day to complete, so even if you are stuck you’ll be able to find a solution; It’s a marathon and rushing to learn faster will not yield in better results. Take your time!

You can always tweet me @theharshdeep or join our Discord Server for help.

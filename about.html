Founder, Heura Media

At Heura Media, I spend time working on WrapitUp - Text Summariser and Medicine Brewery.

WrapItUp - Text Summariser™ is an iOS app that summarises text algorithmically and is used by 3M+ users. With support of over 128 languages, WrapItUp helps you shorten a set of paragraphs to the most relevant sentences containing key points you should be aware of.

Medicine Brewery was an open developer platform that hosted medicine and generic information, pharmacies and hospital datasets over API end points, analytical tools to predict consumption of medicines over time, compute possible diseases affecting an area based on medicine consumption and possibly predict future diseases based on current lifestyle*** Medicine Brewery was later shut down because it was too dangerous to have algorithms this powerful freely available to anyone on the internet.

Read more about it on the Heura Media Blog.
